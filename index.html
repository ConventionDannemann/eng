Dannemann National Convention 2018

It is my great pleasure to invite you to the annual DANNEMANN Convention, which will be held from 12 to 14 April at the DANNEMANN Center in Brissago.

The year that we left behind has been full of successes, satisfactions and results that we can only be proud of.

In 2017 we invited you to be dreamers and not to limit what you can achieve. Today we touch a reality that has gone beyond the dreams of  us all, thanks to the commitment and passion put into practice by each of you, day after day.

Carrying on our values, based on:
trust & responsibility
openness & honesty
development & challenge
continous strive for exellence
we will have laid the foundation to stay focused and clear to our esteemed consumers.

I am convinced that this year we will write History surpassing the 100 million cigarillos sold (250'000kg). Result never achieved in the history of DANNEMANN in Italy.

As was said on the occasion of the celebration for the 40th anniversary of  ITA: "the clocks mark the time, people make history ".
And as a team this year we will make history!

See you soon,
Fernando
